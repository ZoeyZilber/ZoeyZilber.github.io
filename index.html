About Me

I am a third year engineering and theater double major at Smith College. My academic interests are primarily in biomedical engineering but I am open to other areas where I can build on my lab skills, coding and 3D modeling skills as well as my interest in medicine. With regards to theatre, I have experience in lighting, sound, props, set building and design of those four areas.
