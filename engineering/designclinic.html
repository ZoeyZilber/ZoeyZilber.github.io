EGR 422D- Design Clinic Engineering Capstone

Background

A full year senior engineering capstone sponsored by Werfen (a medical device company). Working with two other teammates in order to design a mechanism to open reagent bags in the GEM family of analyzers.

Time Tracking

The expectation of this class is ~10 hours per week and it was a requirement to keep track of that time so that our hours could be submitted in weekly progress reports to our coach. I was also concerned that due to theatre (a department that requires irregular periods of intense commitment), I would have less consistent hours and wanted a way to monitor and assess if I was still meeting the required hours per week.
