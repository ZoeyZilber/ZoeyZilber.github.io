Summer Internship at Park Lab, Harvard School of Public Health

Background

Applied computational analysis of lung epithelial cells undergoing wound repair. Implemented ImageJ to segment nuclei; used MatLab to determine if size, density, etc. of nuclei increased closer to wound site and map their migration during wound healing. Used microscopy to take images of the nuclei to be used in ImageJ.

Work

Primary tasks involved understanding previous lab research, attending lab meetings, staining and running procedures on cells, utilizing ImageJ's tracking feature to create a 3D understanding of cells, programming in MatLab to quantify and graph data on cells, and presenting on the process.
