Summer Stock Audio Assistant at Weston Theater Company

Audio assistant for four shows (two musicals, two plays). Learned how to construct ear rigs, mount antennas, and conduct a line check and scratch test. Acted as an instrument technician for guitars (electric and acoustic) and drum set. Primarily worked as an A2 while intermittently shadowing the A1. Season included Buddy: The Buddy Holly Story, Ur A Good Man Charlie Brown, What The Constitution Means to Me, and Singin' In The Rain.
